# Installed Plugins/Mods/Datapacks

## [**Nickname Plugin**](nickname.html)

- Allows the users to have custom nicknames.

## **Death Count**

- Deaths will be counted and shown in the player list

## **Multiplayer sleep**

- Only one person has to sleep to skip the night

## [**Heads plugin**](heads.html)

- Lets you browse and get decorative heads.

## **Forgetful Vaults**

- Makes Vaults reusable.

## [**Skinrestorer**](skinrestorer.html)

- Adds the option to change your skin directly on the server.

## [**Home plugin**](home.html)

- Lets you create custom home points to teleport to.

## [**Spawn plugin**](spawn.html)

- Adds a spawn teleport command

## [**Just TPA**](tpa.html)

- Adds teleporting to other players

## [**GSit**](gsit.html)

- Adds the ability to sit on certain blocks

## **No creeper block damage**

- Stops creepers from damaging blocks.
